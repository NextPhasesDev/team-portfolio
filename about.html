About NextPhases

A team building scalable digital solutions from Lusaka, Zambia.

Our Story

NextPhases was born from a simple belief: that great software shouldn't be out of reach for startups, freelancers, and growing businesses — especially in our region.

We're a team based in Lusaka, Zambia, combining fresh perspectives with solid technical skills. We build websites, applications, and digital tools that help our clients move into their next phase of growth.

Whether it's a local business needing their first website or a school looking for custom software — we deliver professional quality at fair rates, with clear communication every step of the way.
