Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your information.

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website or contact us, we may collect:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, company name - only when you voluntarily submit them through our contact form.
  • Project details: Information you provide about your project needs, budget, and timeline.
  • Usage data: Basic, anonymised analytics about how you interact with our website (pages visited, time spent). We do not use invasive tracking.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and project requests
  • Provide the services you've requested
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send relevant updates about your project (only if you've engaged us for work)

We will never send you unsolicited marketing emails or share your details with third-party advertisers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to anyone. We may share data only in these cases:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • With service providers who help us operate our website (e.g., form submission services like Formspree), who are bound by their own privacy obligations

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is handled securely using industry-standard practices. Contact form submissions are processed through secure, encrypted channels. We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected.

Our website is hosted on Netlify, which provides SSL/TLS encryption for all traffic. Form data is transmitted over HTTPS.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

Our website uses minimal client-side storage:

  • Theme preference: We store your light/dark mode choice in your browser's local storage so your preference is remembered between visits.
  • No tracking cookies: We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site trackers, or any third-party analytics cookies.
  • No fingerprinting: We do not use browser fingerprinting or any other covert identification techniques.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: For loading our brand typography (Open Sans). Google may collect anonymised usage data — see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Font Awesome (CDN): For loading icons. Served via Cloudflare CDN.
  • Formspree (when connected): For processing contact form submissions — see Formspree's Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at team@nextphases.dev.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. International Users

NextPhases is based in Lusaka, Zambia. If you access our website from outside Zambia, please be aware that your data may be transferred to and processed in Zambia. By using our services, you consent to this transfer. We take reasonable steps to ensure your data is handled securely regardless of where it is processed.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: team@nextphases.dev
  • Location: Lusaka, Zambia
